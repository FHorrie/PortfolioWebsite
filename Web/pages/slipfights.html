SlipFights

Settle lifelong feuds in Cactus Town!

SlipFights is a couch-pvp arena shooter in which 4 outlaws duel it out in Cactus Town.
Pick from a wide arrange of hats to determine your character health and weapon!
Eliminate the correct outlaw on your contract to earn coins!
The outlaw with the most coins wins!

For SlipFights, I programmed movement, UI and I did the game design.
This was hands down my greatest finished project thus far. Together with my wonderful team of 5, we made SlipFights
over the course of 3 months.

Greatest Difficulty

Designing the game was by far the hardest hurdle during this project.
Our initial prototype was rejected, so we had to go back to the drawing board.
We ended up with a detailed blueprint how we wanted our game to feel and play like,
so for our time in production, everything went smooth like butter!
