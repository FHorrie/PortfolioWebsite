My contributions

During my internship, I have worked on Hyper Dash for almost the entire duration.
Most notably, I made many of the systems that support the in-game toys as well as some actual toy functionality.
I also assisted in creating a dynamic object pool system to support custom weapon skins,
as well as overhauling the store, reworking most of the game's diegetic UI
and adding functionality for previewing the weapon skins and toys.
