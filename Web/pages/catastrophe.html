CATastrophe

Throw Cats!

Made for the Mini-Jam: Explosions, CATastrophe is a puzzle game where you launch cats into various structures to knock down all the fish.
The player gets 5 cats with varying abilities to help knocking down the buildings that the fishes rest on.
Make sure you can get all the fish before you run out of cats!

Greatest Difficulty

The time it took designing the game within the Game Jam limits was a catalyst
for being on a very tight scedule during the following development.
We made CATastrophe in 2 days, of which half a day was spent in pre-production.
The game was finished about 3 hours before the deadline.
