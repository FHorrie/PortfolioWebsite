My contributions

My biggest contribution to Forefront was making the scoreboard implementation.
I entered the project in the middle of development. Contrary to different projects I had been a part of,
the Forefront team enforced very strict programming guidelines to ensure other developers can pick up where you left off.
While implementing the scoreboard, core game features like kill/death tracking did not exist yet.
Therefore it was very important to make sure there was a clear entrypoint to update playerdata.
