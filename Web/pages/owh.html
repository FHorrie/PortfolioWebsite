Otters & Wizard Hats

Co-op roguelike with magical otters and funny hats

Otters and Wizard Hats is a 4 player co-op roguelike with a twist.
Players must traverse the magical school in search of a the legendary wizard hat.
During their adventure, the group will fight many enemies in different parts of the school.
Collect different types of hats along the way to grant yourself special effects, but beware!
If all of the otters die, progress is reset!

Greatest Difficulty

The late joining has been the hardest concept we had to introduce into the project.
It is a completely custom implementation with controller device recognition and control mapping at runtime.
