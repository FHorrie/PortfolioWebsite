Droids

A modern take on the classic Atari Asteroids

Made for annual DAE Game Jam, Droids offers a modern take on Asteroids
Find weapon upgrades as you fend off asteroids and alien ships!
Every target hit grants you points. The game ends when you die.
Try to score as high as possible!

Greatest Difficulty

Being the first Game Jam game for all our members, most of the difficulty was in communicating well with each other.
We had to make sure we managed to create as little overlap as possible in order to maximize our development output.
This project tought me why proper commit messages are super important!
