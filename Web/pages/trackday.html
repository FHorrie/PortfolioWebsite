Track Day

Arcade driving sim with simple but detailed physics

Currently being made as a passion project, Track Day will be a racing game for up to 4 players.
Players can choose from a couple of tracks and cars to compete against each other.
It uses custom arcade driving physics to do suspension and driving based on spherecasting.

Car model made by the wonderful Emilija Juknyte

Greatest Difficulty

For this project, most internet resources embed the wheels into the rigidbody.
I made the wheels function separately to allow easy reuse (and the creation of unorthodox car design).
This process was quite difficult.
